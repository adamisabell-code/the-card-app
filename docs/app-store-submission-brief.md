# The Card — App Store Submission Design Brief

Use this document as the source of truth for generating App Store submission assets and copy.

---

## 1) Product Overview

- **App Name:** The Card
- **Tagline:** Tee Party's Scorekeeper
- **Category:** Sports
- **Core Purpose:** Track and score Wolf rounds of golf, generate receipt-style round recaps, and share results.
- **Primary Audience:** Golf groups/foursomes, especially players running Wolf format rounds.

---

## 2) Core User Value

- Start a new round quickly.
- Track hole-by-hole Wolf scoring.
- See winners, spread, and recap at round end.
- Generate a polished "receipt" style summary.
- Share round recaps with friends.

---

## 3) Key User Flows (for visual direction)

### A. Main App Flow (primary)
1. Home screen
2. Tap **Play Your First Round** (or **Run It Back** for returning users)
3. Configure round
4. Play holes / update scoring
5. View recap + receipt output

### B. Preseason First Receipt Flow (secondary, route-only)
- Accessible via:
  - `/first-receipt`
  - `/receipt-kit`
- This flow is intentionally **not** a normal home CTA.
- Intended for QR-based onboarding from mailed First Receipt Kit.

---

## 4) App Personality & Tone

- Competitive but playful
- Premium dark sports aesthetic
- Clean, modern, high-contrast UI
- Language style: short, confident, energetic

Voice examples:
- "Play Your First Round"
- "Run It Back"
- "Earn your receipt. Prove it."

---

## 5) Visual Style Guidance

- **Theme:** Dark mode by default
- **Look:** Sharp, modern, athletic, slightly premium
- **Accent colors:** Cyan/blue + warm orange highlights
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif, strong hierarchy
- **Imagery motifs:** Golf, scorekeeping, receipt metaphor

Avoid:
- Cartoonish style
- Cluttered layouts
- Low-contrast text

---

## 6) Screens to Design for App Store Submission

Design a cohesive App Store screenshot set that tells a clear story:

1. **Home / Entry**
   - Show app branding and primary CTA.
2. **Round Setup**
   - Show player/round initialization.
3. **In-Round Scoring**
   - Show hole-by-hole Wolf gameplay.
4. **Receipt Output**
   - Show receipt card style summary.
5. **Round Recap / Share**
   - Show final results and share-ready recap.

Optional extra:
- **Preseason First Receipt** screen from `/first-receipt` route (as onboarding variant).

---

## 7) Screenshot Caption Concepts (Draft)

Use short, benefit-first copy:

1. "Start your Wolf round in seconds."
2. "Track every hole with confidence."
3. "Keep the whole foursome synced."
4. "Turn every round into a shareable receipt."
5. "Recap winners, points, and spread instantly."

## 10) Constraints & Notes

- Keep the home screen focused on normal app usage (Play Your First Round / Run It Back).
- Do not position the preseason flow as a default home CTA.
- Preseason flow exists for direct URL / QR onboarding.
- Ensure accessibility-minded color contrast and readable caption text.
